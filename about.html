About the Club

Dedicated to the sport of Mogul Skiing in the Southern Alps

Our Mission

The New Zealand Moguls Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering the growth and development of mogul skiing across New Zealand. We provide a platform for athletes of all ages to improve their skills, from grassroots enthusiasts to elite competitors representing NZ on the world stage.

What We Do

Based in the heart of the Southern Alps, we organize high-performance training camps, sanctioned competitions, and community events. Our goal is to create a supportive environment where athletes can master the art of speed, turns, and aerials on the moguls course.

We work closely with major ski resorts like Cardrona and The Remarkables to ensure the best possible facilities for our members. Whether you are looking to take your first jump or aiming for Olympic glory, the NZ Moguls Club is your home on the snow.
