About the Club

Dedicated to the sport of Mogul Skiing across New Zealand

Our Mission

The New Zealand Moguls Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering the growth and development of mogul skiing across New Zealand. We provide a platform for athletes of all ages and abilities to elevate their skills—supporting everyone from grassroots enthusiasts to elite competitors representing New Zealand on the world stage.

What We Do

We organize training camps, sanctioned competitions, and community events designed to progress riders at every stage of their journey. From initial step-ups at local indoor snow facilities to high-performance camps across premier alpine peaks, our goal is to create a supportive environment where athletes can master the art of speed, turns, and aerials on the moguls course.

We utilize top-tier training grounds both across New Zealand and internationally to ensure the best possible environments for our members. Whether you are looking to take your first jump or aiming for world-stage glory, the NZ Moguls Club is your home on the snow.
