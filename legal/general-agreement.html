General Agreement

Terms, Conditions, and Code of Conduct

1. Introduction

This General Agreement (“Agreement”) outlines the combined terms that apply to all members and participants of New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated (NZMC Inc.). These terms include membership obligations, conduct expectations, safety requirements, medical declarations, emergency consent, and media consent.

Definition — “Training Venues”: For the purposes of this Agreement, “Training Venues” refers to any locations used by NZMC Inc. for training or club-related activities, including but not limited to ski resorts, indoor snow facilities, trampoline parks, gymnasiums, sports centres, and any other venues approved by NZMC Inc.

By agreeing to this document, the Participant (or Parent/Guardian for minors) confirms that they have read, understood, and accepted all terms below.

NZMC Inc. is a non-profit organisation operating under New Zealand law.

2. Membership Agreement Acceptance

The Participant agrees to the terms of the NZMC Inc. Membership Agreement, including:

  • Membership responsibilities
  • Payment and refund policies
  • Communication consent
  • Privacy and data protection requirements

3. Code of Conduct Compliance

The Participant agrees to follow the NZMC Inc. Code of Conduct, including:

  • Respectful behaviour toward coaches, participants, and venue staff
  • Compliance with all instructions from NZMC Inc. coaches
  • Safe conduct at all times
  • Respect for all Training Venue rules and policies

NZMC Inc. may remove or suspend participants who violate conduct expectations.

4. Safety Rules

The Participant agrees to:

  • Follow all NZMC Inc. safety instructions
  • Follow all Training Venue safety rules
  • Wear required protective equipment (helmet mandatory)
  • Use equipment responsibly
  • Stay within designated training areas
  • Avoid unsafe behaviour, including high-speed straight-lining or entering restricted zones

Failure to follow safety rules may result in removal from training.

5. Medical Fitness Declaration

The Participant confirms that:

  • They are physically fit to participate in skiing, moguls training, trampoline activities, or any related training
  • They have disclosed all relevant medical conditions to NZMC Inc.
  • They will not participate if unwell, injured, or under the influence of alcohol or drugs
  • Parents/guardians must ensure minors meet these requirements

6. Emergency Medical Consent

The Participant (or Parent/Guardian for minors) authorises:

  • NZMC Inc. coaches or volunteers to provide or arrange first aid
  • Training Venue staff to assist as needed
  • Emergency services to be contacted if required

The Participant accepts responsibility for any medical costs incurred.

7. Media & Photography Consent

The Participant consents to NZMC Inc. capturing photos or videos during training or events and using them for social media, the website, promotional materials, and club communications. Participants may withdraw consent at any time by written request.

8. Accuracy of Information

The Participant confirms that all information provided during registration is accurate and complete, including:

  • Personal details
  • Emergency contact information
  • Medical disclosures

The Participant agrees to update NZMC Inc. if any information changes.

9. Agreement to Policy Updates

NZMC Inc. may update its policies from time to time. Continued participation after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

10. Acceptance

By agreeing to this General Agreement, the Participant (or Parent/Guardian for minors) confirms that:

  • They have read and understood all terms
  • They agree to comply with all NZMC Inc. rules and Training Venue rules
  • They accept the inherent risks associated with training activities
  • They accept all responsibilities outlined in this Agreement
