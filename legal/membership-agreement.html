Membership Agreement

New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated (Non-profit Organisation, Auckland, New Zealand)

1. Introduction

This Membership Agreement (“Agreement”) is a legally binding document between New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated (“NZMC Inc.”, “the Club”, “we”, “our”) and the individual member (“Member”, “you”).

Definition — “Training Venues”: For the purposes of this Agreement, “Training Venues” refers to any locations used by NZMC Inc. for training or club-related activities, including but not limited to ski resorts, indoor snow facilities, trampoline parks, gymnasiums, sports centres, and any other venues approved by NZMC Inc.

By registering as a Member, you agree to:

  • This Membership Agreement
  • The Liability Waiver & Release of Claims
  • The Code of Conduct
  • The General Agreement (Combined Terms)

NZMC Inc. is a non-profit organisation operating under New Zealand law.

2. Purpose of Membership

Membership provides access to:

  • NZMC Inc. community activities
  • Training programs (fees may apply)
  • Club communications
  • Eligibility for club events

Membership does not guarantee access to training unless training fees are paid.

3. Membership Eligibility

You must:

  • Complete registration
  • Agree to all legal documents
  • Provide accurate personal & emergency contact info
  • For minors: obtain parent/guardian consent

NZMC Inc. reserves the right to decline membership applications.

4. Membership Types & Fees

NZMC Inc. may offer:

  • Free membership tiers
  • Paid membership tiers
  • Training session fees
  • Event participation fees

Fees may change with reasonable notice. Membership fees are non-refundable, except where required by law.

5. Member Responsibilities

Members agree to:

  • Follow NZMC Inc. rules and the Code of Conduct
  • Follow coach instructions
  • Respect all Training Venues’ rules
  • Maintain safe behaviour at all times
  • Provide accurate personal information
  • Update emergency contact details when necessary

6. Health & Safety

Members acknowledge:

  • Skiing and moguls training involve inherent risks
  • They must be physically fit to participate
  • They must follow all safety instructions
  • They must wear appropriate protective equipment
  • Unsafe behaviour may result in removal from training

7. Liability & Risk Acceptance

By becoming a Member, you agree that:

  • You participate voluntarily and at your own risk
  • You have read and accepted the Liability Waiver
  • NZMC Inc. is not responsible for injuries arising from skiing, training, or personal actions
  • NZMC Inc. is not liable for loss or damage to personal property
  • Training Venues are not responsible for NZMC Inc.-organised activities beyond their normal duty of care

8. Privacy & Data Protection

NZMC Inc. collects and stores personal information in accordance with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020.

We may collect:

  • Name, contact details, emergency contact
  • Training attendance
  • Photos/videos from club events (with consent)

We will never sell or share your data except:

  • Training Venues for safety purposes
  • Emergency services if required
  • Insurance providers for incident reporting

You may request access to or deletion of your data at any time.

9. Payments, Refunds & Cancellations

  • Training fees must be paid before participation
  • Refunds are only provided if NZMC Inc. cancels an event
  • No refunds for change of mind or non-attendance
  • Multi-session passes are non-transferable unless approved by NZMC Inc.

10. Suspension or Termination of Membership

NZMC Inc. may suspend or terminate membership for:

  • A Member violates the Code of Conduct
  • A Member engages in unsafe or disruptive behaviour
  • A Member provides false information
  • Fees remain unpaid

Members may cancel their membership at any time by written notice.

11. Photography & Media Consent

Members consent to NZMC Inc. using photos or videos taken during club activities for:

  • Social media
  • Website
  • Promotional materials

Members may opt out by written request.

12. Insurance

Members acknowledge:

  • NZMC Inc. carries Public Liability and Professional Indemnity insurance
  • ACC covers personal injury but does not cover liability
  • Members are encouraged to obtain personal sports insurance

13. Amendments

NZMC Inc. may update this Agreement at any time. Members will be notified of significant changes. Continued participation after updates constitutes acceptance.

14. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of New Zealand.

15. Acceptance

By registering, you confirm acceptance of all terms.
