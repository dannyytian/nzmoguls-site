Liability Waiver & Release of Claims

New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated

1. Introduction

This Waiver is between New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated (NZMC Inc.) and the participant.

Definition — “Training Venues”: For the purposes of this Agreement, “Training Venues” refers to any locations used by NZMC Inc. for training or club-related activities, including but not limited to ski resorts, indoor snow facilities, trampoline parks, gymnasiums, sports centres, and any other venues approved by NZMC Inc.

2. Assumption of Risk

I acknowledge:

  • Skiing & moguls training involve inherent risks
  • Risks include falls, collisions, equipment failure, etc.
  • I participate voluntarily and at my own risk

3. Release of Liability

I release:

  • NZMC Inc.
  • NZMC Inc. coaches, volunteers, officers
  • Training Venues and their staff

from all liability for injury, property damage, or loss.

4. Participant Responsibilities

I agree to:

  • Follow instructions
  • Follow Training Venues’ rules
  • Use proper equipment
  • Not participate if unwell

5. Medical Treatment Consent

I authorise first aid and emergency services if needed.

6. Indemnity

I indemnify NZMC Inc. and Training Venues for damages arising from my actions.

7. Minors

Parent/guardian must sign on behalf of minors.

8. Media Consent

I consent to photo/video use.

9. Insurance Acknowledgement

I understand ACC does not cover liability.

10. Governing Law

New Zealand law applies.

11. Electronic Acceptance & Signature

Because this Waiver is completed as part of the online membership registration process, the Participant (or Parent/Guardian for minors) provides their legally binding consent electronically.

By submitting the online registration form and checking the required consent boxes, the Participant acknowledges and agrees that:

  1. Their electronic submission constitutes a valid electronic signature under the New Zealand Electronic Transactions Act 2002.
  2. Their typed name, contact information, and timestamp recorded by the system serve as their electronic signature.
  3. The Waiver is fully enforceable without a handwritten signature.
  4. For Participants under 18, the Parent/Guardian’s electronic signature is required and legally binding.

The Participant (or Parent/Guardian) confirms that all information provided is accurate and that they understand and accept all terms of this Waiver.
