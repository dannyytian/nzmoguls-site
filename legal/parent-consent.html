Parent / Guardian Consent

Authorisation for Minor Participants

1. Introduction

For the purposes of this document, “Training Venues” refers to any locations used by New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated (NZMC Inc.) for training or club-related activities, including but not limited to ski resorts, indoor snow facilities, trampoline parks, gymnasiums, sports centres, and any other venues approved by NZMC Inc.

Participant Information

  • Age

Parent / Guardian Information

  • Relationship to Participant
  • Phone Number
  • Email

3. Consent to Participate

I, the parent/legal guardian of the above-named participant, hereby give permission for my child to participate in all activities organised by New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated (NZMC Inc.), including training sessions, events, and activities held at any Training Venues.

I understand that moguls skiing and related activities involve inherent risks, and I accept these risks on behalf of my child.

4. Acceptance of Club Policies

I confirm that I have read, understood, and agree to the following documents on behalf of my child:

  • Membership Agreement
  • Liability Waiver & Release of Claims
  • Code of Conduct
  • General Agreement (Combined Terms)

I understand that my child must follow all NZMC Inc. and Training Venues’ safety rules.

5. Medical & Health Declaration

I confirm that:

  • My child is physically fit to participate in skiing activities
  • I have disclosed all relevant medical conditions to NZMC Inc.
  • I will ensure my child does not participate if unwell or injured
  • I understand that skiing may cause injuries despite precautions

6. Emergency Medical Consent

In the event of an accident or medical emergency, I authorise:

  • NZMC Inc. coaches or volunteers to provide or arrange first aid
  • Training Venues’ staff to assist as needed
  • Emergency services to be contacted if required

I accept responsibility for any medical costs incurred.

7. Behaviour & Supervision

I understand that:

  • My child must follow coach instructions at all times
  • Unsafe behaviour may result in removal from training
  • NZMC Inc. may suspend or terminate participation for repeated misconduct
  • I am responsible for ensuring my child is dropped off and collected safely

8. Photography & Media Consent

I consent to NZMC Inc. taking photos or videos of my child during club activities for social media, the website, and promotional materials. I may withdraw this consent at any time by written request.

9. Liability Acknowledgement

I acknowledge that I have read and accepted the Liability Waiver on behalf of my child. I understand that NZMC Inc. is not responsible for injuries arising from inherent risks of skiing, and Training Venues do not assume additional liability. I acknowledge that ACC covers personal injury but not liability or property damage.

10. Confirmation

By completing the online registration form, the Parent/Guardian confirms they are the legal parent or guardian, they have read and understood this Consent Form, they agree to all terms on behalf of the minor, and all information provided is accurate.

11. Electronic Acceptance & Signature (Parent/Guardian)

Because this Consent Form is completed as part of the online membership registration process, the Parent/Guardian provides their legally binding consent electronically on behalf of the minor Participant. By submitting the online registration form and checking the required consent boxes, the Parent/Guardian acknowledges and agrees that:

  1. Their electronic submission constitutes a valid electronic signature under the New Zealand Electronic Transactions Act 2002.
  2. Their typed name, contact information, and the timestamp recorded by the system serve as their electronic signature.
  3. This Consent Form is fully enforceable without a handwritten signature.
  4. The Parent/Guardian is legally authorised to make decisions on behalf of the minor Participant.
  5. All information provided is accurate, and the Parent/Guardian understands and accepts all terms of this Consent Form.

The Parent/Guardian agrees that this electronic signature has the same legal effect as a handwritten signature.
