Code of Conduct

New Zealand Moguls Club Incorporated (Non-profit Organisation, Auckland, New Zealand)

1. Introduction

Definition — “Training Venues”: For the purposes of this Agreement, “Training Venues” refers to any locations used by NZMC Inc. for training or club-related activities, including but not limited to ski resorts, indoor snow facilities, trampoline parks, gymnasiums, sports centres, and any other venues approved by NZMC Inc.

2. Purpose

To ensure a safe, respectful environment.

3. Behaviour Expectations

Members must:

  • Act respectfully
  • Follow instructions
  • Avoid unsafe behaviour

4. Safety Requirements

  • Wear helmet
  • Follow Training Venues’ rules
  • Maintain spacing
  • No dangerous actions

5. Training Conduct

  • Enter moguls lane only when instructed
  • No overtaking
  • No high-risk tricks

6. Respect for Others

No bullying, harassment, or discrimination.

7. Equipment Use

Use only approved equipment.

8. Health

Do not participate if unwell.

9. Parents

Parents must ensure minors follow rules.

10. Breaches

NZMC Inc. may remove or suspend members.
